Crowdaoke gets honoured by UNAOC

04 Feb 2013

I'm delighted to report that Crowdaoke, the karaoke platform concept I was working on last year with Helena Puig Larrauri and Heidi Rosbe, received an 'honourable mention' in the United Nations' Alliance of Civilizations' CREATE UNAOC competition.

Thanks to everyone who provided their support and advice during the process, and we hope to be able to work further on Crowdaoke later this year.

In the meantime, best of luck to the five finalists - we're looking forward to seeing the results of their fantastic proposals.
